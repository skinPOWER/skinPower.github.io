Our Advocacy Projects

At skinPOWER, we are dedicated to raising awareness about eczema and supporting those affected by the condition. Our advocacy projects focus on education, awareness, and providing a platform for people to share their stories and find support.

Eczema/Skin Health Education

skinPOWER is dedicated to raising awareness about skin conditions, combating misconceptions, and enhancing knowledge about skin health in our community. Some of our ideas include:

  • Hosting sessions at schools to raise awareness about the mental toll that skin conditions can have.
  • Discussing the importance of a good skincare routine and educating on proper skin care practices.
  • Offering sessions at homeless shelters to share how to care for your skin in difficult circumstances.

We believe these initiatives will educate the community and foster a more informed, compassionate perspective on skin health.
